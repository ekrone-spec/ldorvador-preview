Example itinerary · 7 days

A week in Jewish Curaçao

← Back home

A sample of how a week unfolds. Every journey is shaped to your group, your pace, and the calendar — this is only a starting point.

Day 1

Arrival & the waterfront

Met at the airport and settled into your hotel near the old city. An evening stroll along the Handelskade as the pastel façades light up over the water.

Day 2

The sand-floor synagogue

A private morning at Mikvé Israel-Emanuel, its floor strewn with sand since 1732, followed by the newly expanded Jewish Museum Curaçao.

Day 3

Beth Haim & the Sephardic story

The centuries-old Beth Haim cemetery, and archive time with a community historian for those tracing family lines.

Day 4

The living community

People-to-people encounters — conversations with community members, and a kosher lunch in the Sephardic tradition.

Day 5

Island & leisure

A gentler day: the coastline, the markets, and time to rest before Shabbat.

Day 6

Shabbat by the sea

Kabbalat Shabbat with the congregation, and a hosted Shabbat dinner as the light fades over the harbour.

Day 7

Farewell

A slow morning and private transfer to the airport — leaving as family, not as visitors.
