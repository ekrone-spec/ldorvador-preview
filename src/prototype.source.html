Willemstad · Since 1651

For more than 375 years, Jewish families have prayed, traded, and built a home on this island. We help you walk into that living story — not as a tourist, but as family returning.

Airport welcome and transfers · hand-picked hotels · community-connected guides · custom itineraries · kosher catering in the Sephardic and Ashkenazi traditions.

One island · One story

A short sail from Venezuela, an ocean of history within.

In the heart of Willemstad stands Mikvé Israel-Emanuel — the oldest synagogue in continuous use anywhere in the Americas, its sand floor unbroken since 1732. Everything we plan orbits this square mile of cobalt harbours, ochre walls, and Hebrew inscriptions worn smooth by generations.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is Curaçao easy to reach?
Yes. Curaçao has direct flights from Miami, New York, and Amsterdam, and is served by major carriers. We meet you at the airport and handle every transfer from the moment you land.
Do you cater to kosher and Shabbat observance?
Absolutely. We arrange kosher catering in both Sephardic and Ashkenazi traditions, plan around Shabbat and festivals, and place you within walking distance of the synagogue when you wish.
Is this suitable for older travellers?
It is our specialty. Itineraries are paced gently, distances are short, transport is private and comfortable, and we adapt every day to your energy. Mobility needs are planned for in advance.
Can you plan for a synagogue or private group?
Yes — group planning is at the heart of what we do. We handle room blocks, motorcoaches, group meals, and services for congregations, families, and heritage organisations of any size.
How do payments work?
You reserve your place through us and we send a secure invoice from our trusted booking partner. No card details are ever entered on this website.
